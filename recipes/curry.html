Curry Masala

Home


This is a standard vegetarian curry on a coconut milk basis.

Ingredients

  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • 1 whole onion
  • (optional)3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 paprika
  • batch of broccoli
  • any vegetable of your liking
  • 200 g white rice
  • couple table spoons of olive oil

Steps

  1. Wash rice
  2. Cut vegetables
  3. Pour salted water into the rice cooker
  4. Put Rice into the rice cooker - set to 35 min
  5. Sear onions
  6. Put spices or curry paste into the pan
  7. Add coconut milk
  8. Add remaining vegetables
  9. Let it cook on low heat for about 20 min
